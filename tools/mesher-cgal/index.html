CGAL Meshing Utility - mesher-cgal

Upstream contribution

Certain minor modifications to the CGAL core may be desirable upstream, particularly those relating to surface marking during volumetric meshing. Most aspects of mesher-cgal are context-specific, so unlikely to be suitable for inclusion in the CGAL project, but may be of use to other downstream users, independently.
